The Concept

Six years of stepping
into purpose.

Sneakers carry our stories. Every scuff, every mile, every step forward represents a journey — and for the young people Y-KNOT serves, that journey is just beginning. This year, we celebrate six years of walking alongside our community, lacing up for the next generation, and building something that will outlast all of us.

Dress your best. Wear your favorites. Leave a legacy.

The Evening

A night unlike
any other.

Your support fuels the scholarships, mentorships, and community programs that change the trajectory of young lives across seven Maryland schools. Every ticket, every bid, and every sponsorship dollar goes directly to expanding Y-KNOT's reach so more young people can find their footing and step confidently into their futures.

Dress Code

Black tie or formal attire, elevated with your favorite pair of sneakers. Think tuxedos, gowns, and classic kicks. The more creative, the better — this is your moment to express both elegance and individuality.

Formal Attire + Sneakers Required
